Privacy Policy

At RealHome, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Personal details such as your name, email, and contact information.
  • Property preferences and saved listings.
  • Communications sent to us via contact forms or emails.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send you property listings that match your preferences.
  • Improve our website and user experience based on analytics and feedback.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share it with:

  • Third-party service providers that help us with our services.
  • Authorities or legal entities if required by law.

4. Security of Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically.

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at support@RealHome.com.
